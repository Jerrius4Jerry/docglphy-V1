About Docglyph

Our Mission

Docglyph was created to provide powerful, privacy-focused digital tools that work entirely in your browser. We believe in user privacy, data security, and the freedom to use essential utilities without compromising your personal information.

What We Offer

  • PDF Tools: Merge, split, and compress PDF files without uploading them to servers
  • QR Code Generator: Create QR codes instantly for any text or URL
  • Password Creator: Generate cryptographically secure passwords
  • Passphrase Generator: Create memorable yet secure passphrases

In an age where data breaches and privacy concerns are common, we designed Docglyph to operate entirely client-side. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Docglyph is built using modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. All processing happens in your browser using Web APIs, ensuring fast performance and complete data privacy.
